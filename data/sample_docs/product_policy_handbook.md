# Product & Policy Handbook

This handbook describes NimbusNote Knowledge Base product policies and user-facing rules for demo and testing purposes. All content is fictional and safe for public demonstration.

## Refund Policy

- Eligibility: Refunds are available within 30 days of purchase for standard individual subscriptions if the customer provides evidence of inability to use core features.
- Process: Submit a refund request via the Billing section in account settings or contact support with your order ID. Enterprise purchases follow contract terms and require account manager approval.

## Data Retention

- User Documents: By default, NimbusNote retains user-uploaded documents for active accounts. Inactive accounts are archived after 365 days of inactivity.
- Backups: System backups are retained for 90 days and are used only for disaster recovery.

## Account Deletion

- Self-service: Users can request account deletion from `Settings → Account → Delete Account`. Deletion schedules a permanent purge after 30 days to allow recovery.
- Enterprise accounts: Account deletion for enterprise customers requires coordination with the account admin and completion of data export/transfer steps.

## Document Sharing Permissions

- Owners: Document owners can set sharing to `private`, `team`, or `public` within the workspace.
- Team sharing: Team-level sharing provides read or edit permissions and supports expiration dates for shared links.
